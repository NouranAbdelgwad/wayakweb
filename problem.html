What is the problem that we solve?!

A large number of resources distracted the youth from learning the most important skills... When we asked the youth what are the most important skills that they should learn at our current age, there were 45.6% of them suggested learning programming, 34.7% of them said learning English, 7% said to develop in their mother language “Arabic language” and 12.3% suggested other fields such as graphic design, business,... so, we decided to collect those fields in one application, to make them able to learn the most important skills at our age in an easy and enjoyable way

After the search and asking programmers about the best road for beginners, most of them suggested knowing the basics first, and others suggested determining the major and then learning the basics. so in Wayak APP, we guide beginners to the best road and best ways to show them the right road without wasting their time!

English It's one of the most important language in the world, According to a 2019 survey by Wall Street English, 18% of professionals who have learned English report that they feel happier at work; 12% say they feel happier in general; and half of English speakers earn 25% more because of their language skills. Learning new language is one of many ways to understand everything, therefore, during the learning process, It's often used as a 'common tongue' if neither person is a native speaker.

And since identity is what determines who we are and is indispensable anywhere, therefore, because of our pride in the Arabic language, there will be a special part in the Arabic language aimed at reviving the language, adhering to the heritage, and spreading the correct meaning and sound language to people again.
